Equal Parental Rights

The following is a list of items in which needs to become a bill/law to improve the lives of children in a divorce situation and to alleviate hatred between parents that puts innocent children in the middle creating hatred between the children and a parent. The following should be a bill/law The following shall be void if a different mutual agreement has been agreed upon by both parents. It is proven that kids take at least 2 days to adjust going from house to house, for this a week on week off schedule is best for children, allows them to fully adjust and gives structure.

1. In a divorce where no abuse of any kind is involved both parties without a different mutual agreement between the two parties shall have shared physical and legal custody with equal amount of visitation.

2. When there is provable child neglect, mental, physical, sexual or verbal abuse to the child or other parent by one parent, the abusive parent shall not receive equal amount of physical custody and the other parent shall receive full physical and legal custody. The abusive parent shall seek therapy and not allowed unsupervised physical custody until a professional psychologist appointed by the Court convinces the Court they are no longer harmful to the children.

3. In a divorce where a non biological parent has become a part of the childrens lives where the biological parent is not a part of the childrens lives then the non biological parent shall have shared physical with equal amount of visitation unless otherwise agreed upon by both parties.

3A. In a divorce where a non biological parent has become a large part of the childrens lives where a biological parent is also a large part of the childrens lives, the non-biological parent shall be considered for partial physical custody.

4. In all divorce situations where there is shared legal and physical custody both parties will share the costs 50/50 of all schooling supplies, uniforms (If needed), medical, dental, extra curricular activities, (Any sports they may play, dance, etc...) It will be the sole responsibility of each parent while the child is with them to cover any costs for sitters, camps, food, clothing, etc...

5. In a divorce situation where one parent has full physical and legal custody whether agreed upon by both parties or due to abuse of any kind then the parent with full custody shall receive child support from the other parent according to the child support guidelines from that state.

6. In a divorce situation where one parent was totally reliant financially on the other parent with no abuse, the parent that was reliant shall receive financial assistance from the other parent for 6 months to a year to give them a chance to find employment and be self reliant.

7. In a divorce situation where one parent was totally reliant financially on the other parent with abuse and that parent that was financially reliant was the one abusing shall receive no financial assistance from the other parent.

8. In a divorce situation where one parent does not want to be in the childs life, that parent that chooses to not be in the childs life shall pay child support according to child support guidelines in that state as well as financial assistance to the parent that chooses to be in the childs life.

9. In a divorce situation where one parent uses the child/children as a pawn to get back at the other parent regardless of reasons then that parent relinquishes their custody agreement and the other parent shall receive full physical and legal custody.

10. In a divorce situation where the child/children are in fear of one parent then the Courts shall appoint a licensed psychologist to determine the reason for this fear.

11. In a divorce situation with shared custody one parent cannot place the child on any long term medications without consent from the other parent.

12. In a shared custody agreement if both parents are in a disagreement on any medications then both parents shall seek any testing needed to prove or disprove the medication in question with the cost being shared 50/50 between parents.

13. In a shared custody agreement all medical appointments, school appointments, etc.. the parent in which makes these appointments shall inform the other parent of these appointments either before making the appointment or as soon as the appointments are made.

The above mentioned will create a better more stable life for children to give them a better life. Will bring more parents back into the childrens lives in which they need and deserve. This will also alleviate any hostile situations between the parents in which causes the children to suffer. To make this law will also alleviate any bias in courts towards certain genders. This will create peace between parents not being able to compete against the other by taking the child away from them.

In divorce situations people have lost the ability to think of the children that are getting hurt but instead think about hurting the other parent for what ever reason that caused the divorce. The children are the ones that suffer and have a higher chance to end up as drug addicts, criminals, failing school, etc... all due to the parents vindictive ways.

With this in place it will give children a happier lifestyle with both parents having little to no negativity towards each other due to the parents wont have the choice to try and take a child away from the other parent. The children will have a higher chance to succeed in life.
